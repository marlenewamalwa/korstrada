Our Story

Born from a Love
of Travel

Korstrada began with a simple observation: the most memorable trips weren't always at five-star resorts. They were in cozy cottages, converted farmhouses, and hidden coastal retreats where you felt truly at ease.

Founded in 2019 by travel enthusiasts who grew tired of generic accommodations, we set out to create something different — a collection of handpicked properties where comfort meets character, and every detail is considered.

Today, we work with property owners who share our passion for hospitality, ensuring every home in our collection offers not just a place to sleep, but an experience to remember.

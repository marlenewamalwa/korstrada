Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a property?

+

Browse our collection of properties, select your dates and number of guests, then click "Book Now" on your chosen property. You'll be guided through a simple booking process where you can review pricing and confirm your reservation.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Our cancellation policy varies by property. Most properties offer a full refund if you cancel at least 14 days before check-in. You can view the specific cancellation policy for each property on its listing page before booking.

Are pets allowed at the properties?

+

Pet policies vary by property. Properties that welcome pets will have a "Pet-Friendly" badge on their listing. You can also filter for pet-friendly properties when searching. Additional pet fees may apply.

Do you offer concierge services?

+

Yes! Our concierge team can help you find the perfect property, arrange local experiences, and answer any questions before or during your stay. Contact us via email or phone to speak with a concierge.

How can I list my property with Korstrada?

+

We'd love to hear from you! Email us at partners@korstrada.com with details about your property and photos. Our curation team reviews all applications and will reach out if your property is a good fit for our collection.
